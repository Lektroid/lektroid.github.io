LEK Productions creates original music for games, film, and interactive media, focused on mood, pacing, and narrative impact. Work ranges from atmospheric and minimal scoring through to high-energy electronic and hybrid sound design-driven compositions.

Music is written with the end context in mind, whether that is gameplay systems, cinematic sequences, or linear visual storytelling. The focus is always on creating audio that supports and enhances the experience without overpowering it.

Experience across electronic music production, synthesis, and studio engineering allows for a flexible approach to composition, from fully structured tracks to adaptive, loopable, and scene-based material.

What I Deliver


- Game-ready music (loopable and adaptive where required)
- Cinematic scoring for film and trailers
- Hybrid electronic and atmospheric composition
- Audio tailored to scene, gameplay, or narrative structure

If you are working on a project that requires original music or audio, feel free to get in touch to discuss your requirements.

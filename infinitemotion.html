Infinite Motion has worked professionally in the industry since the early '90s under many guises, releasing his music on every popular medium, he also dipped his toes in the Trance scene in the late '90s under the pseudonym Sola where he remixed for some of the biggest artists on the scene. Having gigged all over the world, he has followed Psytrance & Progressive-Trance steadily over the years. Infinite Motion presents himself firmly in the scene with a fresh identity and brand new uplifting sounds.

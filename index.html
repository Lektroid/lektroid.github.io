LEK Productions is the brainchild of Richard Elliott. A long time producer who has been involved in the music industry since the Summer of Love '89, playing his music in clubs and outdoor events around South-East England. This all took off for him in 1992, where his early releases reached UK national chart status. Over the decades that followed he has been involved in many projects in the electronic music industry with many notable successes.

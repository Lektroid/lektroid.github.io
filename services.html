LEK Productions provides professional audio services for music, film, games, and interactive media, including mastering, composition, sound design, and synthesis-based audio creation. With decades of experience in electronic music and studio production, we focus on delivering high-quality, implementation-ready results.

Mastering
Mastering is the main service we offer, as it is possibly the most vital process of all; it serves to enhance the music you’ve put all your creative efforts into to sound great on all platforms and systems. We take every aspect of mastering very seriously and treat every track respectfully, working closely with our clients, taking into consideration creative choices with the highest regard for exemplary results. Our degree-level mastering is always tailored to your requirements, working on each project individually to the highest standards ready for distribution for both digital and vinyl.

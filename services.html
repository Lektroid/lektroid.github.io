LEK Productions offers degree-level services with over 30 years experience in the industry, such as audio mastering for both digital and vinyl, composition, content creation and sound design for the movie and gaming industries, as well as soundbank and patch creation for both software and hardware synthesizers.

Mastering

Mastering is the final touch on your music before it reaches the listeners ear. We take every aspect of mastering very seriously and treat every track respectfully working closely with you, the client, taking into consideration creative choices with the highest regard for exemplary results. Our degree-level mastering is always tailored to your requirements, working on each project individually until you are 100% satisfied with the end result. Definitive alignment for Album/EP consistency, corrective balance adjustment, stereo width enhancement using mid-side equalisation, then leveling the end result for industry level maximisation whilst retaining essentaial dynamics, using multi-stage processing to bring you the most impact for your productions to stand head and shoulders above the competition.

Composition

Movie and game soundtrack composition is available from LEK Productions. We offer over 30 years experience in the audio industry and treat every project with the appropriate consideration necessary to enhance the final product beyond expectations.

Sound Design

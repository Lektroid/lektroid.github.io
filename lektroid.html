Lektroid has featured on labels such as Street Sounds, Dominance Electricity, Crobot Music, Binalog recordings, Elektrodos and many more. He has gigged all over the world for moderate returns. His benevolence really shines through having donated music to MacMillan Cancer Support charity via the 'Touched Bass' compilation.

Electro-Funk Albums

BEYOND THEORY
Followup album to Quantum Theory. Some of his most inspired work is found on this distinctive album.
QUANTUM THEORY
Lektroid's second full album, his finest works are found here. Some of his most recognised tracks also feature on this album.
ELECTRO FUNK SELECTION
Lektroid's First and foremost album; an onset to all his future classics. An artistic paragon.

Ambient & Chill-out

JOURNEY
An ambient expedition of the mind. After so much interest and enquiries on Youtube and various social media, Lektroid decided to give this awesome track a full release. A real journey!
SERENITY
Melancholic, yet opulent; this ambient piece initially featured on the limited edition Radio Equinoxe compilation and is now available here.
HEARTS OF SPACE
Originally broadcast as a live youtube appearance. Much interest in this ambient piece has given good reason for a full release. This is the full 40 minute recording, unavailable anywhere else.
ASTRAL
Originally written in 1989, this is one of Lektroid's early examples of an ambient odyssey. This ageless piece holds up to today's standards unimpaired.

Founder producer of The Brainstorm Crew, featured on Whitehouse Records, F-Project and Formation Records in the early '90s. His updated pseudonym, Brainstormer has featued on many vinyl releases in the Breakbeat Hardcore scene, here he brings you some of the freshest uplifting Breakbeat & DnB grooves, as well as many unreleased gems written through the decades.

9T2 EP
6-Track EP featuring remixes of original tracks from 1992 and also unreleased gems. There was a very limited blue vinyl release of this with only 300 copies (no represses), this digital release includes 2 bonus tracks not featured on the vinyl.
POWER WITHIN
Post Milennium Breakbeat Pressure. This album was compiled with music from Brainstormer's most captivating tracks over the years, many unreleased, making this one special album. A must have in every Breakbeat lover's collection!

Founder producer of The Brainstorm Crew & Wise Ones, featured on Whitehouse Records, F-Project and Formation Records in the early '90s. Now, as a solo artist, his updated pseudonym Brainstormer has featured on many vinyl releases in the Breakbeat Hardcore scene, with an ever-growing presence within the Drum & Bass community. Here he brings you some of the freshest DnB grooves, as well as many unreleased gems written through the decades, taking you all the way back to the roots of the sound.

The links here point to Bandcamp, where it provides the most support for the artist. For those who prefer their usual stores, the tracks are also offered on Beatport, Juno, Apple Music, and many other platforms. Every purchase helps keep the music alive and is greatly appreciated.

OMNISCIENCE EP
The magnificent Brainstormer returns to Omni Music with 5 more deep breakbeat cuts. The title track starts the EP with wistful piano and chopped breaks that spring to life with energetic bass and floating strings. Voyager continues with the tight break work as sprinkles of chords and synths gradually build to a momentous breakdown. Grafix changes the mood with a rolling beat as oldskool bass faces off with chord stabs and uplifting strings and piano. Fragments keeps the uplifting feelings as chopped breakbeats and melodics swirl around a rolling amen that propels the track deeper into musical bliss. The downtempo Echoes finishes this excellent new EP with oldskool breaks and dub interspersed with rapturous synths, a perfect end to this new collage of sounds.
SACRED SHORES EP
Omni Music is proud to welcome Brainstormer to the label. Being a stalwart of the scene for such a long time it's a joy to bring his uplifting vibes to Omni fans. Known for his work from the early 90's on labels such as Formation, F-Project, Whitehouse and more, he has tirelessly kept his productions evolving through time with a host of pseudonyms and styles. He turns his expert hand to atmospheric DnB for Omni Music. The title track starts the late summery vibes rolling breaks, joyous synths, sunny piano and hypnotic drops of vocals. Signal Path is up next with more beguiling melodic hooks but on a more frenetic vibe as pounding bass and thumping breaks propel the track forward. Island Mist keeps the classic breaks flowing as it rolls effortlessly with double bass and enchanting synth hooks. The EP ends on the downtempo breaks of Rollin' the Dice a serene head-nodding journey through lands of synths, arpeggios and oldksool bass stabs.

The Final Stage

Mastering is the final step before your music reaches the listener. It refines balance, clarity, depth, and impact, ensuring consistent translation across all playback systems.

Mastering is not a fix for poor mixing. It is a precision process that enhances what is already working, adding cohesion, control, and polish while preserving the intent of the original production.

Each master is created with careful attention to translation, loudness, stereo image, and spectral balance. Projects are handled using multi-stage processing and prepared to meet current industry standards for both digital and vinyl formats.

How to submit your music

Please provide clean unmastered copies of your mixdowns with no processing on the master bus.

A text file with the following:
  • Song Title(s)
  • Artist Name
  • Genre

Also, if you could provide any of the following:

  • BPM
  • ISRC codes
  • Artwork
  • Remixer, publisher, or any other relevant info

Output levels should not exceed -0.3 dB at any point. Audible clipping cannot be corrected during mastering and sufficient headroom is required for optimal results.

Before submission, ensure channels that do not require sub frequencies have appropriate low-cut filtering applied. This helps maintain clarity in the low end and preserves headroom for punch and loudness in the final master.

All bit depths and sample rates are accepted. Files are normally delivered in their original format unless conversion is specifically requested.

When you’re ready to submit your pre-masters, please use the contact form to begin the process. Once your enquiry has been received, submission details, including file transfer instructions, will be provided.

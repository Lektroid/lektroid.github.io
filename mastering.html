The Final Touch That Makes Your Music Shine...

Mastering is one of the most critical stages of the music production process. It's the final step that transforms your creative work into a polished, professional sound ensuring it shines on any platform, from streaming services to high-end sound systems. Often misunderstood as a quick fix for a bad mix, the true purpose of mastering is to enhance an already solid mix, adding the final touches that elevate it to its fullest potential - bringing out excitement, clarity, detail, openness, and balance, all ready for distribution.

As the last step before your music reaches the listener’s ears, mastering ensures your track sounds incredible on every system, whether it’s played through a phone speaker, a high-end stereo, or a major event sound system. I take this responsibility seriously, treating every track with the respect it deserves. I work closely with my clients, always considering their creative vision, while delivering exceptional results with precision and care.

My degree-level mastering service is bespoke, tailored to your specific needs. Each project is approached individually, ensuring that I meet the highest standards for both digital and vinyl formats. I take pride in making sure you're 100% satisfied with the final result.

From ensuring consistency across albums or EPs to spectral balancing, stereo field enhancement, and industry-standard loudness, I use multi-stage processing to maximize the impact of your music. My goal is always to ensure that all my work meets the highest industry standards for clarity, balance, and consistency. While I work quickly, I never compromise on quality. Every track is treated with the attention and expertise it deserves, resulting in a master that truly reflects your artistic vision.

How to submit your music

Please provide clean unmastered copies of your mixdowns, ie. no plugins on the master bus.

A text file with the following:
  • Song Title(s)
  • Artist Name
  • Genre

Also, if you could provide any of the following:

  • BPM
  • ISRC codes
  • Artwork
  • Remixer, publisher, or any other relevant info

Output levels should not exceed -0.3dB throughout the mix (Audible peaks will not be magically hidden by the mastering process). This will allow enough headroom to deliver outstanding results.

In preparation for submitting your mixdown, please ensure that all channels not requiring sub frequencies (such as lead riffs, hi-hats, snares, etc.) have either a low cut filter applied or the low shelf reduced. This will help eliminate any unnecessary bass frequencies, preventing muddiness in the low end and providing additional headroom for optimal loudness and punch in the final mix.

I can work with any bitrate and sample depth. While sample rate conversion is possible, to avoid potential aliasing during the process and to ensure optimal quality, we typically deliver the file in its original format unless otherwise requested.

When you’re ready to submit your pre-masters, get in touch via our contact form and I’ll provide details on the next steps, including using WeTransfer and any additional information required.
